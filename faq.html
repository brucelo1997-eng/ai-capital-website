Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any coding experience to take your courses? ▼

Absolutely not! Our courses are designed for complete beginners with zero coding experience. All our courses use a no-code approach with visual interfaces and intuitive tools. We'll teach you everything you need to know to build powerful automation workflows without touching a single line of code.

How long does it take to complete a course? ▼

Our courses are designed to be completed in 3-6 weeks depending on the level. The OpenClaw Beginner course runs for 4 weeks with 2-hour sessions on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday evenings. However, once you enroll, you get lifetime access, so you can learn at your own pace and revisit materials whenever you need to.

What tools will I learn to use? ▼

Our courses focus on OpenClaw and Claude, which are powerful no-code automation platforms. These tools allow you to build sophisticated automations without any programming knowledge. They're designed for professionals and are used by companies of all sizes to save time and increase productivity.

What if I'm not satisfied with the course? ▼

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee with no questions asked. If you're not completely satisfied within 30 days of enrollment, simply let us know and we'll refund your entire course fee. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we stand behind the quality of our teaching and materials.

Do I get lifetime access to course materials? ▼

Yes! Once you enroll, you get lifetime access to all course materials, videos, and resources. You can revisit lessons anytime, and as we update our courses with new content and features, you'll have access to all updates automatically at no additional cost.

Is there an active community I can join? ▼

Absolutely! All enrolled students get access to our private community forum where you can ask questions, share projects, and connect with other learners. We also host monthly office hours with instructors and have a supportive environment where everyone is encouraged to help each other succeed.

What kind of support is available if I get stuck? ▼

We provide comprehensive support including live Q&A sessions during class, access to a private community forum, email support from our team, and monthly office hours with instructors. Whether you have a technical question or need clarification on a concept, we're here to help you succeed.

Will this help me in my career? ▼

Definitely! Automation skills are in high demand and increasingly valuable in the job market. Our graduates report saving significant time at work, being more productive, and in many cases, becoming the "automation expert" at their organization. These skills make you more valuable to employers and open up new career opportunities. You'll also build real projects during the course that you can showcase in interviews or to clients.
